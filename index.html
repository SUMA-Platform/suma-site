Sustainability-as-a-Service

SUMA Platform is a sustainability-focused Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solution, designed to assist businesses and ecosystems towards net-zero emissions.

Our platform empowers businesses in calculating their carbon footprints, decarbonizing their activities, and ensuring effortless compliance with the latest regulations. In addition, we facilitate carbon offsetting with carbon credits that actively remove carbon from the atmosphere. Reach net-zero with us.
